The Story of NestoArt

NestoArt, a brainchild of the prodigiously talented Ankita Agarwal, emerged when she was a mere 14 years old. This venture manifested from a young girl's fervent passion for beauty, space, and transformative design. Ankita, even during her early years, showcased an intrinsic talent for breathing life into spaces, making them more than just areas—they became experiences.

"Every design is not just a project but a narrative, a story, a piece of 'art' that speaks of the person residing in it."

The inspiration behind the name "NestoArt" encapsulates the essence of building a 'nest'. Our designs resonate with the intimacy, comfort, and personal touch that a nest symbolizes. Today, our family at NestoArt continues to grow, with a broadened vision and an even greater commitment to our craft.

Our devoted team, comprising professionals, artists, and designers, pledges that every project is an emblem of our client's desires, dreams, and our unwavering commitment to aesthetic appeal and quality.

Our Unwavering Mission

At NestoArt, it's more than just designing spaces—it's about creating stories, sculpting journeys. Our mission is deeply rooted in the belief that every corner we design should echo the soul of its inhabitant. Every element, be it a brick or a shade of color, is meticulously chosen to reflect personal journeys, tastes, and aspirations.

Our Vision for the Future

As we look forward, our path is illuminated by a singular vision. NestoArt aspires to become a touchstone in the world of interior design. More than our innovative and unique designs, we wish to be known for our dedication to understanding and mirroring the individual essence of every client in the spaces we curate. Every project is a fresh canvas, every design, a new adventure.
